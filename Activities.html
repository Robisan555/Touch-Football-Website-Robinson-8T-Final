Touch Football Drills and Skills




Passing Skills

A couple of individual passing skill techniques. Make sure to perform for the whole 50 seconds for consistancy in performance.


Defence and Evasion Skills

Defence and evasion exercises for touch football. A little bit of equipment is needed, including a partner.


Performing a Wrap and Cut

A more specific skill, the wrap and cut. It is a useful skill in game situations. Equipment includes a partner and some cones.






Warm Ups




General Warm Up

A general warm up is very beneficial to your body to prevent injury. It increases blood flow to the muscles and raises your body temperature.


Lower Body Warm Up

Quick warm up drills for your legs. There are various excersises. No equipment needed for this exercise.


Upper Body Warm Up

A short dynamic warm up for the upper body with commentary and an example. No equipment needed except for a chair to sit on.






Activities




Sprint Training

A high intensive touch football based sprinting session with warm ups at the start. No equipment needed except for an open space.


Full 20 Min Workout

General strength and full body workout for 20 minutes. No equipment required except for a mat.


Fun Fitness Activities

Fun fitness activities to do with a team or family members. Activity requires cones and must be completed on a flat surface.
